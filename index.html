learn a new language by listening to podcasts

by Shulem Leiser april 28 2022

how many times have you wanted to improve your English but didn't succed? how many times have you thought "i wish i could take part in this conversation but they talk too fast? i'm sure many of you have had this struggle. i know, i too have had it for many years"

that's why i've developed what i call the stream method. a method where you train your brain to get used to a new language the same way babies learn their mother language. babies do not read dictionaries, nor do they go to special classes. Babies learn a language by getting exposed to it all trhe fucking time.

In this sense, podcasts are far better than any other form of media. they have no background noise / music / or any form of distraction. moreover, it is very easy to change the speed of playback as well as skip back if you didn't understand something

that's why iv'e handpicked for you a list of easy to listen to podcasts where the hosts speak in an easy to understand level and at an average speed. let's get going!
